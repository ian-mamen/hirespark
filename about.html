About HireSpark

HireSpark is an Earthworks Machinery leasing Company founded in 2020

by four like minded enterprenuers who joined together and helped

establish the company which has become a household name in Kenya.



HIRESPARK holds itself to the highest standards, over the period we have undertaken very challenging projects in very hostile grounds within the country. We are happy to report that our commitment to projects remain solid.




WHO WE ARE

HIRESPARK remains true to the principles it was founded on. The core principles being efficiency, cost friendliness and time reliability.

With the experience we have, we have gained the capabilties to understand how different projects can be implemented using the latest technology machineries.

Our rental solutions allows customers to redirect their capital towards investment in the assets that appreciate rather than assets that drop in value quickly, while at the same time spreading the cost of rental equipment over its useful life.

Our asset portfolio ranges from infrastructure to motor vehicles.

HireSpark has an expansive global footprint, providing rental finance solutions directly to a global customer base comprising large and medium size organisations. We have developed innovative specialised rental solutions encompassing a wide range of equipment types.
